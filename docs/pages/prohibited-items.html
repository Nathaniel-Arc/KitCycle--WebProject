Prohibited Items

Items that may not be listed or rented on WMSU KitCycle.

Listing prohibited items will result in immediate account suspension and may be reported to the WMSU Office of Student Affairs. Repeat offenders face permanent ban.

Dangerous & Illegal Items

Weapons, knives, or any bladed instruments (beyond academic use)
Explosives, firecrackers, or flammable materials
Controlled substances, drugs, or drug paraphernalia
Alcoholic beverages and tobacco products
Counterfeit or stolen goods

Privacy & Safety Risks

Hidden cameras, GPS trackers, or surveillance equipment
Lock-picking tools or unauthorized access devices
Personal documents, IDs, or credential forgery tools

Health & Hygiene

Used personal hygiene items (toothbrushes, razors, etc.)
Prescription medications or medical devices requiring authorization
Perishable food items

Intellectual Property

Pirated software, movies, or music (physical or digital)
Photocopied textbooks violating copyright law
Fake or knock-off branded electronics

What IS Allowed

KitCycle is built for legitimate academic and student needs:

  • ✅ Scientific calculators, drafting tools, rulers
  • ✅ DSLR cameras, tripods, lighting equipment
  • ✅ Laboratory gowns, goggles, and safety equipment
  • ✅ Textbooks and reference materials (original copies)
  • ✅ Laptops, tablets, and portable chargers
  • ✅ Art supplies, easels, and drawing tablets

Spotted a prohibited listing? Report it: report@wmsukitcycle.edu.ph
