Terms & Conditions

1. Rental Agreement

All rental transactions are agreements between the faculty and the student. Equipment must be returned in the same condition as received.

2. Payment Terms

Rental fees are calculated per day/hour as set by the faculty. Advance payment may be required before item pickup.

3. Lost or Damaged Items

Lost items must be replaced or fully compensated at current market value. Damaged items will be assessed for repair costs.

4. Platform Responsibility

WMSU KitCycle acts as a facilitator and is not liable for disputes between users. All exchanges must occur at designated campus meetup locations.
