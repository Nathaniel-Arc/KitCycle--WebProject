Terms of Service

Please read these terms carefully before using the WMSU KitCycle platform.

1. Acceptance of Terms

By accessing or using WMSU KitCycle, you agree to be bound by these Terms of Service and our Privacy Policy. This platform is exclusively for enrolled Western Mindanao State University (WMSU) students, faculty, and staff with valid institutional credentials.

2. Eligibility

  • You must be a currently enrolled WMSU student or active faculty/staff member.
  • You must provide a valid WMSU email address (@wmsu.edu.ph) for verification.
  • You must be at least 18 years of age or have parental consent on file.
  • Accounts are subject to identity verification within 24–48 hours.

3. Rental Agreements

All rental transactions are agreements between the lender (item owner) and the borrower (renter). WMSU KitCycle acts as a facilitator and is not a party to the rental contract. Users are responsible for:

  • Accurately describing item condition, availability, and pricing.
  • Returning items on time and in the same condition as received.
  • Compensating lenders for any damages, loss, or late returns.
  • Completing all exchanges at designated WMSU campus meetup locations.

4. Prohibited Conduct

  • Using the platform for commercial resale or non-academic purposes.
  • Listing prohibited, stolen, or counterfeit items.
  • Creating fake accounts or misrepresenting your identity.
  • Conducting meetups off-campus or at unauthorized locations.
  • Harassing, threatening, or intimidating other users.

5. Dispute Resolution

In the event of disputes between users, WMSU KitCycle may act as a neutral mediator. All decisions made by the KitCycle Administration team are final. Unresolved disputes may be escalated to the WMSU Office of Student Affairs.

6. Limitation of Liability

WMSU KitCycle is provided "as is" without warranties. We are not responsible for the quality, safety, or legality of listed items, nor for any loss or damage arising from rental transactions between users.
