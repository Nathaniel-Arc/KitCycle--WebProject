Privacy Policy

How WMSU KitCycle collects, uses, and protects your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

When you create an account, we collect the following:

  • Identity Data: Full name, WMSU student/employee ID, profile photo.
  • Contact Data: WMSU email address, phone number (optional).
  • Usage Data: Browsing activity, items viewed, rental history.
  • Transaction Data: Rental requests, meetup locations, and communication logs.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To verify your identity as a WMSU community member.
  • To facilitate rental transactions and campus meetups.
  • To calculate and display trust scores and ratings.
  • To send notifications about rental updates and platform announcements.
  • To improve platform functionality and user experience.

3. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information:

  • All data is transmitted over HTTPS (SSL/TLS encryption).
  • Passwords are hashed using bcrypt and never stored in plain text.
  • Access to personal data is restricted to authorized administrators only.
  • Regular security audits are conducted by the WMSU IT department.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data to third parties. Limited information may be shared with:

  • Other KitCycle users (name, rating, verification status) to facilitate trust.
  • WMSU administration when required for safety or disciplinary matters.
  • Law enforcement if required by Philippine law (RA 10173 — Data Privacy Act).

5. Your Rights

Under the Data Privacy Act of 2012 (RA 10173), you have the right to:

  • Access and review your personal data stored on the platform.
  • Request correction of inaccurate information.
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data.
  • Withdraw consent for data processing at any time.

6. Cookies & Local Storage

KitCycle uses browser localStorage to maintain your session state, preferences, and saved items. No third-party tracking cookies are used. You can clear this data at any time through your browser settings.
