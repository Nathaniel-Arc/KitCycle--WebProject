Verified Sellers Program

Every lender on WMSU KitCycle undergoes identity verification. Only verified WMSU community members can list items — ensuring safe, trusted transactions on campus.

How Verification Works

1

Create Account

Register with your official @wmsu.edu.ph email and upload your student or faculty ID photo.

2

Identity Check

Our team cross-references your ID with WMSU records. This takes 24–48 business hours.

3

Start Listing

Once verified, you earn the Verified Badge and can list items for rent immediately.
