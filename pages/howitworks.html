How KitCycle Works

Simple, safe, and built for the WMSU community. Start renting or lending in minutes.

For Lenders

Earn money from items you already own

1

List Your Gear

  • Take clear photos of your item
  • Write a detailed description
  • Set your daily or weekly rental price
  • Specify pickup location on campus
2

Get Requests

Receive rental requests from verified WMSU students on campus

  • Get notified when someone requests your item
  • Review the renter's profile and ratings
  • Accept or decline requests
  • Communicate pickup details
3

Meet on Campus

Arrange safe, convenient handoffs at designated campus locations

  • Meet at designated safe zones
  • Verify item condition together
  • Complete the rental agreement
  • Return item at agreed-upon time

For Renters

Access the gear you need without breaking the bank

Browse Items

Search through hundreds of available items

Request to Rent

Send a request to the item owner

Schedule Pickup

Arrange a convenient meeting time

Get Your Item

Meet on campus and start using the item
